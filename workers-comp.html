What's Worker's Compensation Insurance?

An insurance policy that protects your employees in case they ever get sick or injured because of work. The policy pays out to cover your employee’s medical expenses and lost wages—and it might even cover ongoing financial and medical support if the injury is serious.

Pay medical expenses for workers who get hurt on the job.

Pay for the missing wages of employees when they get injured.

Protect yourself against lawsuits and legal fees.

Do I need Worker's comp

If you live in any of these states, yes.

Worker's Compensation insurance is MANDATORY in nearly every state in the country. In CA, if you don't buy Worker's Comp, you face up to $100,000 and a year in jail with penalties!

Even if you are an independent contractor, you will likely need Worker's Comp. As per CA's ABC ruling and new state laws, many states will consider contractors as employees for the sake of Worker's Comp.
