Commercial Auto Insurance

If you operate a vehicle as part of your business, the law requires Commercial Auto Insurance. If you are transporting cargo and it is damaged or stolen en route, or a traffic accident should occur during business operations, your personal auto insurance will not cover you.

Speak to an agent today about coverage for your commercial vehicle, including but not limited to the following:

Expenses in the event of theft of or damage to vehicles

Legal expenses and coverage of uninsured motorists
